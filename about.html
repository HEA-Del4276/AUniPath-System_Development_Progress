About Us

AUniPath is a website designed to help senior high school students in the Philippines choose the right college course, university, and scholarship based on their academic track and interests. It provides organized and reliable information on degree programs, schools, and scholarship opportunities, along with updates on entrance exams and academic events. The platform aims to prevent course mismatches, a common issue caused by lack of guidance, which often leads to academic struggles or dropout.

Why Use AUnipath?

At AUnipath, we believe that every learner deserves a clear and guided path toward their dreams. That's why we've created a platform that simplifies your journey to higher education—whether you're looking for the right course, an ideal school, or life-changing scholarships.

Here's what makes AUnipath your ultimate academic companion:

  • School Finder with Purpose – Explore schools that align with your needs, values, and career aspirations—whether local or international.
  • Scholarship Opportunities Within Reach – We connect you to scholarships you actually qualify for—because funding should never be a barrier to education.
  • Guided and Personalized – We're not just a directory—we're your partner in progress. Our smart tools and friendly support help you navigate your path with confidence.
  • Accessible for Everyone, Anywhere – Whether you're a student, a working professional, or a curious learner, AUnipath brings opportunities to your fingertips—right when you need them.

Course Finder

The Course Finder section is designed to address the widespread issue of strand–course misalignment in the Philippines. Many students enter college without a clear understanding of which degree programs are aligned with their SHS strand or personal interests. This feature allows them to explore recommended courses based on their strand, helping them avoid unnecessary academic struggles, wasted tuition, or program shifting later on.

What the Course Finder solves:

  • 1. Mismatch between a student’s SHS strand and the college course they pursue.
  • 2. Lack of awareness about which college degrees are best suited to their background.
  • 3. Risk of taking bridging courses, shifting programs, or dropping out due to poor course fit.

University Finder

The University Finder is a key feature of AUniPath that helps senior high school graduates find universities that align with their academic background, interests, and chosen course. Many students feel lost when exploring their options, especially when information online is unorganized or incomplete. This section addresses that confusion by offering students a way to explore universities based on relevant filters such as location, course offerings, entrance exam requirements, and scholarship availability.

What the University Finder solves:

  • 1. Difficulty finding universities that match the student’s SHS strand and intended course.
  • 2. Lack of centralized information about admissions, programs, and scholarship options.
  • 3. Overwhelm caused by scattered or outdated content from social media and unofficial sources.
