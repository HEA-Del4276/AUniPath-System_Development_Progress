About Us

AUniPath is a website designed to help senior high school students in the Philippines choose the right college course, university, and scholarship based on their academic track and interests. It provides organized and reliable information on degree programs, schools, and scholarship opportunities, along with updates on entrance exams and academic events. The platform aims to prevent course mismatches, a common issue caused by lack of guidance, which often leads to academic struggles or dropout.

Why Use AUnipath?

At AUnipath, we believe that every learner deserves a clear and guided path toward their dreams. That's why we've created a platform that simplifies your journey to higher education—whether you're looking for the right course, an ideal school, or life-changing scholarships.

Here's what makes AUnipath your ultimate academic companion:

  • School Finder with Purpose – Explore schools that align with your needs, values, and career aspirations—whether local or international.
  • Scholarship Opportunities Within Reach – We connect you to scholarships you actually qualify for—because funding should never be a barrier to education.
  • Guided and Personalized – We're not just a directory—we're your partner in progress. Our smart tools and friendly support help you navigate your path with confidence.
  • Accessible for Everyone, Anywhere – Whether you're a student, a working professional, or a curious learner, AUnipath brings opportunities to your fingertips—right when you need them.
